# Privacy Policy for Thai Swimmer Ranking

    Effective Date: May 20, 2026

    Thai Swimmer Ranking ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you
  use our mobile application.

    ---

    ## 1. Information We Collect
    We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
    *   No Accounts: The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or log in.
    *   Local Storage: All data regarding your "Team" or "Followed Swimmers" is stored locally on your device using a private SQLite database. We do not have
  access to this data.

    ## 2. Third-Party Services
    The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

    ### A. Google AdMob (Advertising)
    We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free users. AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising ID to provide personalized
  ads.
    *   Google Privacy & Terms: https://policies.google.com/privacy

    ### B. Google Play Billing (Subscriptions)
    We use the standard Google Play Billing system to manage Pro subscriptions. Payments are handled securely by the Google Play Store. We do not store or
  have access to your credit card information.
    *   Google Play Terms of Service: https://play.google.com/intl/en-US_us/about/play-terms/

    ### C. TAA Public Data
    The App fetches public swimming rankings and results from the official Thailand Aquatics Association (TAA) website. We do not transmit any of your
  personal data to TAA.

    ## 3. Data Security
    Since all your team data is stored locally on your device, the security of that data depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-
  level encryption and screen locks.

    ## 4. Children’s Privacy
    Our App is designed for swimmers of all ages, including children. Because we do not collect any personal information, accounts, or contact details, we
  are compliant with standard child privacy regulations (COPPA).

    ## 5. Changes to This Policy
    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

    ## 6. Contact Us
    If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: narinster@gmail.com
